On    the  North    American      continent
afore 1600          approximately
a freedom  adequate approximation existed.

Credit, debt,  money                were not empowered.
All     unused items when requested were     given.
To      jobs   persons              did  not daily commute.
Importance           when accorded
tasks   were              completed.
The     outcome      and
the     reward       were tantamount.
Enslavement          was  abstained.
As      desired persons   moved.
Travel  passes, visas,    were  not demanded.

Around  1600 the      Europeans     arrived.
Atrocities,  disease  and manifest  destiny were brought.
On the North American continent the freedom ended.
