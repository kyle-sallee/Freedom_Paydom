On  paydom worlds  slave      culture exists. Anxiety   and fear flourish.
In  slave  culture compliance and   herd      mentality become.
The nail   that    protrudes  until flush     is  pounded.

On  freedom worlds    liberty        culture       exists.
To  do   as self      pleases                  is  liberty.
To  be      amusing,  appreciated,   eccentric is  desired.
The appropriate       behavior       idea      is  abstained.
To  be      hyper,    mellow,        silly     are fine.

To  paydom  minds the liberty        culture   is  befuddling.
All non     atrocious behavior                 is  fine.
By  young   minds     liberty        culture   is  emulated.
The goals     while   attaining
and           when    attained       happiness is  expressed.
For happiness the     anticipation             is  abstained.
The happiness in      most   moments already       exists.
The happiness to      attain the     liberty   is  sustained.
The culture   the     life   each    moment    is  happiness.
To  awake,    being,  learn, attain  each mind is  eager.
