A job and wage slavery are tantamount.

By  paydom minds the  lethal deprivation to mitigate jobs  are   wanted.
By  technology   jobs when   eliminated  or exported slave minds complain.

On      freedom worlds
liberty since   sustained
slavery is      abstained.
A   job         is  not desired  and  can not be inflicted.
A   worthy goal is      desired.
The worthy goal when    attained many desirable  feelings result.
Importance to   accord  as       self desires    since    sustained
a   meaningful  life                  can        be       attained.
