In    the paydom  upon culpable    persons
atrocity, persecution, prosecution is    inflicted.

In    the freedom      liberty     since sustained
atrocity, persecution, prosecution is    abstained.

By slave  minds  culpability and   causality as  different  is      asserted.
By free   minds  culpability and   causality as  identical  is      asserted.

In  the   paydom culpability since denied    the atrocities are not precluded.
In  the  freedom culpability since admitted  the atrocities become  precluded.
