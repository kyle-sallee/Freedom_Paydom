for the     other  team        ball      deprivation is  intended.

and for     non    compliance  deprivation           is  inflicted.

By  the     games  the         slave     mentality   is  solicited.

goal attainment, creativity, giving, and receiving are emphasized.
The  rule        creation    is          abstained.
The  goals                   are         asserted.
Any  atrocity    infliction  is tacitly  undesirable.
The  games       names          while    similar
the  play        styles         differ!
The  liberty     mentality   is solicited.
